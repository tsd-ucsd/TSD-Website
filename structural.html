Jordan Locke


Structural Lead

Hello! My name is Jordan and I’m a fourth-year structural engineering major here at UCSD. I’m a huge fan of enjoying the outdoors, through hiking, biking, camping, and just generally hanging out with the flora and fauna. Because of this, I’m also super passionate about sustainability, and saving our environment both to protect the species that thrive here (including us!) and to make sure we can continue appreciating our natural world. I joined Solar Decathlon to meet like-minded people -- those combining their love of the outdoors with their interest in engineering and design -- and get a taste of what I hope I can spend the rest of my life doing: sustainable design!

Laura Kemble


Laura Kemble is a third year structural engineering major in Seventh College, she is also a part of the UCSD women’s rowing team. Her main reason for joining Triton Solar Decathlon was the sustainability aspect, she believes that students today have the power to change our future for the better and that TSD is really taking initiative on that front. Laura’s interest in this competition consists of the use of renewable energy, the construction of the structure, and presenting the final product to the judges.
