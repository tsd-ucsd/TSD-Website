Interest Form

Architecture

The architecture team is responsible for designing the structure in the form of the building shell and general layout along with developing the overall interior aesthetic function of the ADU.

Electrical

Our team focuses on efficiency, performance, analysis, management, design, and integration of solar and renewable energies.

Finance + Marketing

The market and finance team provides financial modeling and budget breakdowns for the team, as well as analyzes market conditions that directly affect the feasibility of the housing concept.

Mechanical + Plumbing

The mechanical and plumbing team incorporates innovative mechanical, HVAC, and water management systems to improve the comfort, efficiency, and sustainability of the ADU.

Media

The media team works to maintain all media platforms, including this website!

Structural

The structural team is tasked with creating a structurally sound and efficient unit design. They are responsible for sourcing local and sustainable materials as well as ensuring that the structure of our ADU is simultaneously code-compliant and innovative in regards to sustainability, affordability, and adaptable housing design.
